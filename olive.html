Olive Health app

User flows

As I was working on the design of this app I wanted to keep in mind the goals of the company while trying to provide for the users needs in the app. I aimed for a design that would help make peoples lives easier and allow scheduling, paying for appointments and contacting doctors. As I was working on this project I learned that finding doctors could be an annoying task for users and this app would be very helpful but their real issues with health had to do with not knowing as much as they would like to know about it. Wanting to do more and learn more for themselves but not knowing how to start. So as I continued my designs of the app I added in some features to help users understand their health better. I added a way to explore health and track their health.
